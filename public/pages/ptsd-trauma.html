Understanding PTSD & Trauma

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is a mental health condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. While trauma can have lasting effects, recovery is possible with proper support.

Common Symptoms

Intrusive

  • Intrusive memories or flashbacks
  • Nightmares about the event
  • Severe emotional distress at reminders
  • Physical reactions to triggers

Avoidance & Mood

  • Avoidance of reminders/places
  • Negative changes in thinking
  • Feelings of guilt, shame, or anger
  • Emotional numbness or detachment

Arousal

  • Heightened reactions or hypervigilance
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Trouble concentrating
  • Irritability or aggression

Healing Strategies

Professional Support

  • Seek help from a therapist
  • Join a support group
  • Consider trauma-informed therapies

Grounding

  • Practice grounding techniques
  • Mindfulness and relaxation exercises
  • Deep breathing when triggered

Self-Care

  • Regular physical activity
  • Consistent sleep routine
  • Connect with supportive people
  • Avoid alcohol and drugs
