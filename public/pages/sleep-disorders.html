Sleep Health & Disorders

Quality sleep is essential for mental and physical well-being. Understanding sleep disorders and practicing good sleep hygiene can transform your health.

Common Issues

Insomnia

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Waking up often during night
  • Waking up too early
  • Not feeling rested

Daytime Effects

  • Excessive daytime sleepiness
  • Irritability or mood changes
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Decreased performance

Other Signs

  • Loud snoring (Sleep Apnea)
  • Restless legs sensations
  • Teeth grinding
  • Irregular sleep patterns

Sleep Hygiene Tips

Routine

  • Stick to a sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime ritual
  • Avoid naps late in the day

Environment

  • Keep room cool and dark
  • Invest in a comfortable mattress
  • Reduce noise
  • Limit screen time before bed

Lifestyle

  • Limit caffeine and alcohol
  • Exercise regularly
  • Manage stress
  • Get natural light exposure

Need Professional Help?

If sleep problems persist or affect your daily life, consult a doctor or sleep specialist.
