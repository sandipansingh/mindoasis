Understanding Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder is a chronic mental health condition causing significant mood swings, affecting sleep, energy, and behavior. With proper management, individuals can lead fulfilling lives.

Common Symptoms

Manic Episodes

  • Increased energy and activity
  • Euphoria or feeling "high"
  • Racing thoughts
  • Irritability
  • Decreased need for sleep

Depressive Episodes

  • Persistent sadness or emptiness
  • Low energy and fatigue
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Feelings of worthlessness

Cognitive & Other

  • Trouble concentrating
  • Difficulty making decisions
  • Changes in sleep patterns
  • Changes in appetite

Management Strategies

Routine

  • Maintain a strict sleep schedule
  • Keep a regular daily routine
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Engage in regular exercise

Planning

  • Identify and plan for triggers
  • Track your moods daily
  • Avoid alcohol and drugs
  • Prioritize self-care

Support

  • Seek professional help
  • Stick to treatment plans
  • Build a support network
  • Communicate with loved ones
