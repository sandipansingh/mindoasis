Stress Management

Stress Management involves using techniques like exercise, mindfulness (deep breathing, meditation), prioritizing sleep, healthy eating, and strong social connections.

Common Symptoms of Chronic Stress / Overload

  • PERSISTENT SADNESS
  • LOSS OF INTEREST
  • FATIGUE
  • SLEEP/APPETITE CHANGES
  • DIFFICULTY CONCENTRATING
  • FEELINGS OF WORTHLESSNESS
  • THOUGHTS OF DEATH OR SUICIDE
  • TALK TO SOMEONE YOU TRUST

Helpful Tips to Manage Stress

  • Practice the 4-7-8 deep breathing technique.
  • Schedule 30 minutes of uninterrupted exercise daily.
  • Use mindfulness or meditation apps (e.g., Calm).
  • Set clear boundaries between work and personal life.
  • Prioritize getting 7-9 hours of sleep nightly.
