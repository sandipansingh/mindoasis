Stress Management

Stress is a normal part of life, but chronic stress can take a toll on your health. Learning to manage it is key to maintaining balance and well-being.

Signs of Overload

Physical

  • Headaches or muscle tension
  • Fatigue and low energy
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Digestive issues

Mental

  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Constant worrying
  • Forgetfulness
  • Racing thoughts

Emotional

  • Irritability or short temper
  • Feeling overwhelmed
  • Anxiety or nervousness
  • Mood swings

Management Strategies

Mindfulness

  • Practice 4-7-8 deep breathing
  • Use meditation apps (e.g., Calm)
  • Practice progressive muscle relaxation
  • Take mindful breaks

Active Living

  • Schedule 30 mins of exercise daily
  • Get outside for fresh air
  • Prioritize 7-9 hours of sleep
  • Eat a balanced diet

Balance

  • Set boundaries between work/life
  • Learn to say "no"
  • Make time for hobbies
  • Connect with friends
