Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural response to stress, but when it becomes overwhelming or constant, it can affect daily life.

Common Symptoms

  • Excessive worrying or fear
  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Racing thoughts
  • Rapid heartbeat or shortness of breath
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Trouble concentrating

Helpful Tips to Manage Anxiety

  • Practice deep breathing or mindfulness exercises
  • Limit caffeine and sugar intake
  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule
  • Engage in physical activity
  • Break problems into small, manageable steps
  • Talk to someone you trust
