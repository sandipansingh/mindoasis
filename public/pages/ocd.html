Understanding OCD

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) involves unwanted intrusive thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions). It's a treatable condition, not a personality quirk.

Common Symptoms

Obsessions

  • Fear of contamination
  • Unwanted forbidden thoughts
  • Need for symmetry/order
  • Aggressive thoughts

Compulsions

  • Excessive cleaning/washing
  • Ordering and arranging
  • Repeated checking
  • Compulsive counting

Impact

  • Severe anxiety or distress
  • Time-consuming rituals
  • Interference with daily life
  • Avoidance of triggers

Management Strategies

Therapy

  • Exposure & Response Prevention (ERP)
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Consult a specialist

Mindfulness

  • Practice observing thoughts
  • Resist the urge to perform rituals
  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Stay in the present moment

Support

  • Join an OCD support group
  • Educate family and friends
  • Be patient with yourself
  • Celebrate small wins
